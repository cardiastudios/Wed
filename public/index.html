About Cardia Studios

Digital Wedding Invitations Made for Beautiful Online Sharing

Cardia Studios designs custom digital wedding invitations and online wedding invitations for couples who want a refined, mobile-friendly alternative to printed cards. Each invitation becomes a private wedding website that guests can open instantly from WhatsApp, Instagram, email, or a QR code.

As a digital wedding invitation designer, Cardia Studios can personalize your invitation with RSVP tracking, guest links, photos, music, maps, schedules, countdowns, dress code details, and custom sections designed around your celebration.

Online RSVP Tracking

Collect guest replies through your invitation link and keep attendance, plus-ones, and family responses organized.

Custom Invitation Design

Personalize names, date, venue, wording, photos, colors, music, timeline, maps, and special wedding details.

Easy Guest Sharing

Send your online wedding invitation by WhatsApp, email, Instagram, or QR code with no app download required.

How Our Online Wedding Invitations Work

01

Choose a design

Browse our exclusive templates and pick the one that speaks to your love story. Each design has an ID: A for Best Sellers, B for Book Invitations, and C for Classic Invitations.
